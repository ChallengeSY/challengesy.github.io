Katane Blitz gains Coprime expansion pack (Oct 4, 2025)

Katane Blitz grows again with the Coprime Pack. This further increases the variety of modules, allowing for an even richer Endless experience.

Nu Replayer leaves beta at last! (Feb 1, 2025)

Nu Replayer receives the last of the enhancements and stabilization currently available to allow it to leave beta.

Although triumphant after 3 years of beta (and 10 years of alpha), support will likely remain ongoing. We look forward to integrating support for black holes and neutron star clusters, as was advertised in the presentation last December.

CSYience Solitaire Series update (Dec 25, 2024)

What a fitting way to end the year, by giving a present/update to CSYience Solitaire. It is now possible to play a series of 3 games back-to-back, complete with a skill level selection that signficantly alters the way a series is played.
