New Nebunoid campaign: Fusion Designs (Nov 24, 2023)

Nebunoid has been on a content update spree. After gaining 2 new official campaigns earlier in the month, Fusion Designs has become its newest addition.

Electric Recharge is a themed/artistic official campaign (20 new levels). Endless Shuffle is the new 100% reward of the game (reuses all of the included levels). Finally, Fusion Designs is a hybrid official campaign (14 new levels + new level variation).

CSYience Solitaire Wizard unleashed (Nov 17, 2023)

CSYience Solitaire has received the biggest update yet: An extensively customizable Solitaire Wizard

This powerful tool allows building nearly any custom solitaire game. Just fill out a form (feeding it a bunch of parameters), and it will generate a new game in the process.

Katane Blitz released! (Oct 20, 2023)

Katane Blitz, a fast paced web remake of Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes, is now playable online.

Practice any of the 11 vanilla modules with Mission mode, or try your hand at Endless mode. We also have a cheat sheet that can be used to read additional resources and speed up play.
