Auxilliary News

Nevergalaxy back online (Dec 11, 2022)

Nevergalaxy is back online. Its sub-projects have returned, with the exception of the pre-designed course leaderboards.

Ionic Merge Labs upgraded and split (Nov 22, 2022)

Ionic Merge Labs has received a significant upgrade to justify splitting it as a separate project from Campaign Tech Simulator.

Most notably, it is now possible to import Ion Storms from any Planets Nu game, and apply their data directly to the simulation.

Campaign Tech Simulator overhauled (Nov 16, 2022)

We have overhauled the Campaign Tech Simulator, taking advantage of the remote data retrieval system to automatically fetch updated data from Planets Nu's server.

The 12 main modules benefit from this overhaul. The Space Dock bonus material (formerly the Parts Builder) also benefitted from this overhaul.

Wrapsweeper released! (Oct 29, 2022)

We welcome Wrapsweeper to the list of projects! Wrapsweeper is a Minesweeper with wraparound borders.

Differentiation Challenge remastered! (Apr 16, 2022)

After being originally delayed for nearly a year and a half thanks to web host snafus, we have released a new... or, rather, remastered project on this site! Welcome back, Differentiation Challenge!
