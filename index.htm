Auxilliary News

Katane Blitz released! (Oct 20, 2023)

Katane Blitz, a fast paced web remake of Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes, is now playable online.

Practice any of the 11 vanilla modules with Mission mode, or try your hand at Endless mode. We also have a cheat sheet that can be used to read additional resources and speed up play.

New SE4X playthrough posted (Sept 1, 2023)

What better way to celebrate a new month than to post a new playthrough of the Space Empires 4X board game. We have also condensed the name of the collection slightly, to improve flexibility for future sub-projects.

CSYience Solitaire expanded (Jul 11, 2023)

CSYience Solitaire has been expanded. Originally comprised just of Golf solitaire, the project has been expanded to now feature 5 different games.
