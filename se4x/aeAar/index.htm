Alien Empires Summary

Due to the huge size of the Alien Empires playthrough, we have decided to break the playthrough down into chapters.

Chapter 1 - Early Economics

This chapter has been tailored to introduce new / less experienced players to the board game. As a result, it has been given the greatest amount of detail over the course of just 2 economic phases.

Chapter 2 - Pre-launch gameplay

This chapter contains economic phases 3 - 7, ending just after the Alien Players finally launch their fleets.

Chapter 3 - The battle for deep space

This chapter contains economic phases 8 - 11, ending with attempting the first assault to the Green Alien Player homeworld of Chulak.

Chapter 4 - The assault of Chulak

This chapter contains economic phases 12 - 17, covering continued assaults of the Green Alien Player homeworld of Chulak.
