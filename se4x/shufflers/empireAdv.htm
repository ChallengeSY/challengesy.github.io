Instructions

This Shuffler allows for the creation of nearly any deck of empire advantage cards. Select a preset or design your own deck, build a single or double deck (with the latter causing twice as many cards to be generated), and then deal away as deep space is explored. Though overkill (only used at game start), this program still saves your stock of cards from one session to another.
