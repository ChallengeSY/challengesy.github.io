Welcome to the Space Empires Resourcepedia, a supplementary collection of resources designed to augment the experience of the board game and related games.

Sub-project List

Battle Simulators

This collection supports near-through simulations of a player fleet versus one or more environmental opponents. Doomsday Machines and Replicator Solitaire are currently supported.

Replay Center

This program allows viewing playthroughs in a more interactive format. The oldest playthroughs have been migrated, and more playthroughs are occasionally added.

Talon Fleet Builder

Although very loosely related to Space Empires by itself, it is possible to design your own fleet for almost any Talon battle. It is also possible to set up for an Empire War.

Unique Ship Designer

This program allows easily designing your own Unique Ship, as if you were playing a Close Encounters expansion game.
