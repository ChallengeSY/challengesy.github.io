Welcome to CSYience Solitaire, an HTML5 solitaire site! No downloads required; just play!

Available Games

Klondike

Klondike is a classic game. Deal 1-3 cards from the stock pile at a time to waste. Secure all 52 cards in foundations to win.

FreeCell

FreeCell is a moderate game. Although movement capacity is limited, open cells and empty tableau piles can be used to aid in movement. Secure all 52 cards in foundations to win.

Spider

Spider is a long hard game. The goal is to set up 8 single-suit King-Ace piles. Black Widow (move by rank) and Tarantula (move by color) are also available.

Golf

Golf is a short, but hard, game. The goal is to remove 35 cards from the tableau, using up to 16 cards in the stock pile.

Yukon

Yukon is a moderate game. Any number of face up cartds can be moved at a time. Secure all 52 cards in foundations to win.

Blackjack

Although not truly a solitaire game, Blackjack uses the same engine. The player can compete with 1 or more hands against the dealer, in an attempt to go as far as they can in a part Arcade part Tournament setting.
