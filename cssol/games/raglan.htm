Instructions

Raglan is a solitaire card game using a deck of 52 playing cards.

The aces are taken out of the deck, meaning that only 48 cards are shuffled. 42 of these cards are dealt to the tableau, with each pile getting progressively larger (but only 7 cards maximum per pile). All cards dealt this way are face up. The remaining six cards are put into the reserve pool, which are not reusable.

In this game, the tableau piles are to be built down by alternating colors. Cards are moved singly, so multiple cards may only be moved after one or more tableau piles are empty. Empty piles can be filled with any card

The foundations are built up by suit. The game is won when all cards end up in the foundations.
