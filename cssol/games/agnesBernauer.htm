Instructions

The tableau is dealt identically to Klondike, except that all cards are face-up. Then a card is placed in the first of the four foundations, acting as the base rank for the other foundation piles. Wrapping is permitted as necessary

Then a row of seven (7) cards are dealt to the reserve pool, with each card belonging to its own reserve.

The tableau piles are built down by alternating colors, and builds may be moved as desired. Empty columns may be filled by the rank just below the base rank (Example: If a game's base rank is Queen, then Jacks may be the only ranks used to refill an empty column)

The remaining cards form the stockpile, which the player goes through one row at a time onto each reserve slot, obstructing any leftover cards as necessary.
