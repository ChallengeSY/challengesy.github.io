Instructions

Yukon is a version of solitaire similar to standard Klondike solitaire. However, groups of cards can be moved regardless of whether the group forms a proper build or not. Yukon also lacks a stock pile. All cards are dealt at the beginning; however, some are face down.

A harder variation, called Russian Solitaire, is also supported.
