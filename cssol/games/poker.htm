Instructions

The objective is to arrange the 25 cards (dealt from a single deck) to form high scoring hands. These are the only cards that are in play.

Hands are scored horizontally, vertically, and diagonally; using the British system:

  • One Pair (1): Two cards are the same rank
  • Two Pairs (3): Each of two sets of two cards are the same rank
  • Flush (5): All five cards are the same suit
  • Three of a Kind (6): Three cards are the same rank
  • Full House (10): Three of a Kind and One Pair in the same hand
  • Straight (12): Cards are in a sequence
  • Four of a Kind (16): Four cards are the same rank
  • Straight Flush (30): Straight and Flush in the same hand

The game is won if $120 can be secured.
