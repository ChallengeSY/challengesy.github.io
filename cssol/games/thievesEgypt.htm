Instructions

Thieves of Egypt is a dual-deck Klondike-type solitaire card game. Deal cards in such a manner that a pyramid is formed using ten piles. Add 1 card to each of the last five piles. All cards dealt this way are face up.

Like Klondike, tableau piles are built down by alternating colors. Foundations are built up by suit. Empty columns may be filled only by a king. Only builds may be moved

The stock is dealt one card at a time. The player may go through the stock a maximum of two times
