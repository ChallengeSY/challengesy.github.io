Project Cleansing Title Pack

Project Cleansing is a Title Pack for ShootMania Storm; containing a cooperative game mode, dubbed Cleansing.

The objective of the Cleansing team (human players) is to capture all goal posts before time expires. There are a few complications; most goal posts are guarded by turrets, and gold nuggets (acquired from dedicated dispensers) are also required to capture a goal post. Finally, if the difficulty is high enough (35%+), there can be a finite number of shared revives that will prohibit further respawns from this team once exhausted.

The Infested team (computers only) is comprised of smaller toads, common 1-armor units that spawn frequently; and cyborgs, durable boss bots (50 - 200 armor) that are resistant to off-zone, but will go down permanently once their armor reaches zero.
