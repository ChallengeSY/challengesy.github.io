Space Yard

Campaign Tech Simulator

Campaign Tech Simulator is designed to simulate tech purchase costs, allowing Planets Nu players to deduce whether they can afford a given combination before commiting their Megacredits. It also contains a variety of bonus materials.

CSYience Solitaire

CSYience Solitaire is a small collection of solitaire card games that are played online. No downloads are required to play this HTML5 project!

Differentiation Challenge

Differentiation Challenge tests the derivative skills of its players. No downloads are required in this remastered project!

Ionic Merge Labs

Formerly a bonus module, Ionic Merge Labs simulates moving and merging of two ion storms, plus has the ability to import ion storms from any Planets Nu game.

Katane Blitz

What if the Keep Talking bomb defusal game was made into a fast paced blitz title? Score points and gain time by defusing modules, but watch out! Time out or exhaust your life pool, and your bomb will blow up!

Nebunoid

Break blocks, collect capsules, clear campaigns in Nebunoid. Breakout-type games are common, but we bring our own uniqueness to the table in the form of variations that can be enabled on a per level basis.

Nevergalaxy

Nevergalaxy is a collection of custom content and smaller projects all grouped together. Enjoy Neverball Clockwork and Puttworks Courses!

Nu Replayer

Nu Replayer is designed to view turns of completed Planets Nu games and see how the starmap actually develops over the course of the game. This project is currently in Beta.

Space Empires Resourcepedia

The Resourcepedia is a supplementary collection of resources designed to augment the experience of the SE4X board game, and related computer games.

Wrapsweeper

Wrapsweeper is a Minesweeper-like game that always enforces wraparound. Harder to play, but less luck required. No downloads are required in this project!
